Step‑by‑Step: How to Switch

1. Decide on Carrier & Phone

  1. Choose between:
    • Verizon + iPhone 14/15 (best home coverage), or
    • T‑Mobile Essentials 55+ + iPhone 14/15 (best price, great in town).

2. Go to the Carrier Store (or Order Online)

  1. Bring your current AT&T flip phone and a photo ID.
  2. Tell them you want to:
    • Keep your current phone number.
    • Move to an unlimited plan.
    • Get an iPhone on a monthly installment.

3. Number Transfer & Setup

  1. The new carrier will transfer your number from AT&T (this is free).
  2. They will activate the new iPhone and make sure calls and texts work. (Bring your Apple ID and Password)
  3. They can help move contacts from the old phone if needed.

4. Learn the Basics on the iPhone

  1. Have them show you:
    • How to make and receive calls.
    • How to read and send text messages.
    • How to adjust volume and ringer.
    • How to charge the phone.

5. Cancel Old AT&T Plan (If Needed)

  1. Once the new phone is working and your number is transferred, AT&T service will usually stop automatically.
  2. Check your final AT&T bill to confirm there are no remaining charges.
